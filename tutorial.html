Quickstart

The purpose of this project is to transcribe key data within an image taken from the British Library's collection of historic playbills. On the following page you will be presented with an image and asked to transcribe a particular category of data (title, actor etc.). Once you have finished, click the Save button to move on to the next task.

Viewer Controls

The following controls are provided towards the top left of the viewer:

  • Zoom in
  • Zoom out
  • Reset zoom
  • Fullscreen
  • Image quality
