Quickstart

The purpose of this project is to transcribe key data fields within an image taken from the British Library's collection of historic playbills. On the following page you will be presented with an image with a highlighted area identifying a particular category of data (title, actor etc.). Your tasks is to transcribe this highlighted data.

Further guidance for each category is presented with each task.

Viewer Controls

The following controls are provided towards the top left of the viewer:

  • Zoom in
  • Zoom out
  • Reset zoom
  • Fullscreen
  • Image quality
  • Toggle selection
